Frequently Asked Questions
How does Omnix protect employees who don't use a Dashlane vault?
+
Omnix is deployed company-wide and protects every employee by default—whether or not they are actively using a Dashlane vault or logged in to their account.
How does Omnix detect phishing attacks?
+
Dashlane Smart Extension uses a proprietary AI model to automatically analyze 75+ traits on web pages—including URLs, page content, and images—providing real-time alerts to help employees avoid phishing risks.
Can Omnix integrate with existing security tools?
+
Yes, Dashlane Omnix integrates with your existing infrastructure and tech stack, including SSO, SCIM provisioning, and SIEM tools.
