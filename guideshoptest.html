Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment to visit the Scottsdale Guideshop?
+
Walk-ins are always welcome at our Scottsdale Quarter location! However, we highly recommend booking an appointment for dedicated 1-on-1 time with a Guide, especially for suit fittings or wedding parties.
Can I walk out with the clothes I try on?
+
Bonobos Guideshops operate as an e-commerce showroom. You try on samples to find your perfect fit, and we place the order for you in-store. Your items will be shipped directly to your home or office, usually within a few days.
Where is the best place to park at Scottsdale Quarter?
+
The closest parking to our store is the North Garage off of 73rd Place. Parking is free for all Scottsdale Quarter visitors.
