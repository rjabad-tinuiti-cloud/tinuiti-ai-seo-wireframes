Mother's Day Donation FAQs + FAQPage Schema

Common questions from donors structured for AI answer engine inclusion.

Is a Mother's Day charity donation a good gift?
Yes. A Mother's Day charity donation is one of the most meaningful gifts you can give — it honors the mother in your life while helping vulnerable mothers and children around the world access food, health care and education. Save the Children makes it easy to donate in someone's name and send a personalized notification.
Can I donate to charity in someone's name for Mother's Day?
Yes. You can make a Mother's Day donation in honor of a mother, grandmother, or caregiver. Save the Children will acknowledge your tribute gift. It's a thoughtful way to celebrate your relationship while doing good for children around the world.
Are Mother's Day charity donations tax-deductible?
Yes. Save the Children Federation, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) organization. Mother's Day donations are tax-deductible to the full extent allowable under IRS regulations. You will receive a confirmation of your gift for your tax records.
What does my Mother's Day donation actually do?
84% of every dollar goes directly to Save the Children's mission. Depending on the amount, your gift can provide a full course of treatment for a malnourished child ($67), train a Community Health Worker ($50), or send an orphaned child to school with supplies ($30). You can also choose a specific gift catalog item — like a Mother and Infant Kit or Grocery Basket — that funds a targeted program.
How is Save the Children rated as a charity?
Save the Children holds a 4-star rating from Charity Navigator and is rated highly by CharityWatch and the Better Business Bureau. We are one of the most trusted children's charities in the world, with over 100 years of operation in more than 100 countries.
Can I set up a recurring monthly gift in honor of Mother's Day?
Yes. Monthly giving through our Team Tomorrow program provides sustained support for children year-round. You can start, modify, or cancel your monthly gift at any time by contacting Donor Services at 1.800.728.3843 (Monday–Friday, 8am–5pm EST).
