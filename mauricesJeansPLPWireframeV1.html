Maurices Jeans Fit Guide: How to Choose Your Perfect Pair
+

Jeggings: When you want the body-hugging stretch of leggings but the structure and coverage of jeans. Fitted from waist to ankle.

Skinny Jeans: Flattering on every body type. More structured than jeggings, highlighting curves while providing a classic denim feel.

Bootcut: Universally flattering, giving a long-legged look. Fits closely through the hip and thigh, opening slightly from knee to ankle.

Curvy Fit: Designed with more room in the hips and thighs, and a contoured waistband to eliminate gaping.

Frequently Asked Questions about Maurices Jeans
+
What sizes are available for maurices jeans?
Maurices jeans come in regular sizes 0-20 (XS-XXL) and plus sizes 14W-24W, with various inseam lengths from extra short (28 inches) to extra long (36 inches) to ensure the perfect fit.
How do I find my correct size using the maurices jeans size chart?
Refer to the maurices size chart for measurements like waist and hip. Measure yourself and match to the closest fit for the best comfort.
